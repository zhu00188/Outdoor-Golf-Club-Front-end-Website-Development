How We Became Golf Space

2022

Club Founded

Golf Space was established with a vision to create a modern, inclusive golfing destination where members could enjoy championship-level play and lasting friendships.

We launched our Golf Academy to nurture new talent and refine the skills of seasoned players through personalized instruction and innovative training programs.

Our community expanded with certified professionals and dedicated members who uphold the highest standards of sportsmanship and performance.
