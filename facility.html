Member Lounge & Event Spaces

Enjoy the social heart of Golf Space — where dining and celebration come together. Our elegant restaurant and private event spaces are designed for both comfort and sophistication. Whether it’s a post-game meal, a weekend brunch, or a milestone celebration, you’ll find the perfect setting here.

We offer breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus, along with a curated beverage list and seasonal chef’s specials featuring local ingredients. Members can reserve our stylish Party Center for private gatherings, tournaments, or corporate events, complete with catering services and customized décor.

Wellness & Recreation

At Golf Space, wellness is an essential part of the golfing lifestyle. Members can unwind by the pool, refresh in our modern locker rooms, or stay active in the fitness zone — all designed for balance and comfort.

The outdoor swimming pool offers a peaceful escape surrounded by greenery, while our locker rooms provide secure storage and premium amenities for your convenience.

Our fitness zone features advanced equipment and personalized training support to help you build strength, flexibility, and confidence — both on and off the course.
